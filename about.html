About Me

My journey as a passionate self-taught developer from Ethiopia

My Story

Hello! I'm Nebil Shebab, a passionate and driven self-taught web developer from the beautiful country of Ethiopia. My journey into the world of web development began with a simple curiosity about how websites work, and it has evolved into a deep passion for creating digital experiences that make a meaningful impact.

Growing up in Ethiopia, I discovered early on that technology has the incredible power to connect people across borders, solve complex problems, and create opportunities where none existed before. Despite facing challenges with limited resources and infrastructure, my determination to learn programming never wavered.

My educational journey has been entirely self-directed, relying on online tutorials, comprehensive documentation, open-source resources, and countless hours of hands-on practice. Through sheer persistence and dedication, I've mastered HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and various modern web technologies. Every line of code I write and every project I complete is an opportunity to learn something new and push the boundaries of what's possible on the web.
